About Me

Hey! My name is Monkendrick Williams. Thanks for checking out my page. This place will continue to evolve over time, so feel free to check back in every once in awhile and see what's new. For now this serves as my portfolio, showcasing some of the skills I have learned.

More About Me

My journey first started as a Combat Engineer in the US Army. I was on year nine and was coming up on the end of my contract. I knew that if I signed another contract I would do the whole twenty years. I opted on taking a chance outside of what I knew and decided not to re-enlist. After that I worked in the banking world until I was able to land my first job within tech. I started as a Helpdesk Analyst for Moore & VanAllen, a big law firm here in Charlotte, NC. Helping users with their software issues daily only fueled my curiosity on how applications were created.

Feeding into this curiosity, I enrolled at CareerFoundry in their Full-Stack Web Developer program. Where I focused and gained skills using the MERN stack. I am also currently finishing up a cybersecurity cohort with Rapid Ascent. Working my way to hopefully become a Cybersecurity Engineer. I want to be able to continue build applications but have a focus of defending software and applications against the threats of the cyber space.

Aside from the work side of things, I really enjoy sports, go Cowboys and Hornets! I enjoy traveling to different cities and discovering their craft beer and food scene. Which I plan to pick up on some personal blogging eventually.
